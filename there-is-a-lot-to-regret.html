There is a lot to regret

even after nearly a year has passed.
There are memories,
burned into my brain,
that time cannot erase or fade.
There are people I have hurt,
friends I have lost,
perhaps forever.
There’s the feeling of guilt
I carry with me
wherever I go,
whenever I see
wounds, inflicted by myself
that I cannot heal.

~ MØNØ
September 12, 2022
